# Business Insights 360

##  Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a **comprehensive analytics solution** for an e-commerce business. It enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions across multiple domains such as finance, sales, marketing, supply chain, and executive strategy.

The dashboard is structured into **six main views**:

* Home View
* Finance View
* Sales View
* Marketing View
* Supply Chain View
* Executive View

---

##  Features & Views

### 1.  Home View

* The main contents page through which other views can be navigated.
* Purpose: Quick snapshot for **all users** before diving into details.

---

### 2.  Finance View

* Focused on **financial performance** and profitability analysis.
* **KPIs displayed:**

  * Net Sales
  * Net Profit %
  * Gross Margin %
  
* **Visuals:**

  * Profit & Loss (P\&L) statement dashboard
  * Sales Performance over time
  * Contribution by product category & region
* Purpose: Helps the finance team **track profitability and cost efficiency**.

### 3.  Sales View

* Detailed breakdown of **sales performance**.

* **Visuals:**

  * Key metrics by customer
  * Key metrics by products
  * Sales by region and country
* Purpose: Supports the sales team in **identifying growth areas and weak points**.

### 4.  Marketing View

* Evaluates the **effectiveness of marketing campaigns**.
  
* **Visuals:**

  * Key metrics by products
  * Key metrics by region
  * Performance matrix
* Purpose: Enables the marketing team to **optimize campaigns and budgets**.

---

### 5.  Supply Chain View

* Tracks **logistics and inventory performance**.
* **KPIs displayed:**

  * Forecast accurracy
  * Net error
  * ABS error
 
* **Visuals:**

  * Mertics by customer
  * Metrics by products
  * Accuracy / Net error trend
* Purpose: Helps operations team **optimize inventory**.

---

### 6.  Executive View

* Designed for **CXOs and senior leadership**.
* **KPIs displayed:**

  * Revenue Growth % by Division and Channel
  * Net sales
  * Gross margin %
  * Net profit %
  * Forecast accuracy
* **Visuals:**

  * Key insights by sub-zone
  * Market share trend
  * Yearly trend by GM%, Net profit%, PC Market share%
  * Top 5 customers and products by revenue
* Purpose: Provides executives with **insights for decision-making and strategy**.
